Application Process Guidance

Most agricultural schemes require the following documents:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Land Records (Khatauni/Khata/Khasra)
  • Bank Account Details (linked with Aadhaar)
  • Passport-size Photographs
  • Mobile Number (for OTP verification)
  • Soil Health Card (for certain schemes)
  • Crop Sowing Certificate (for insurance schemes)
  • Caste Certificate (for SC/ST specific benefits)

  1. Identify the Scheme: Select the appropriate scheme based on your farming needs
  2. Check Eligibility: Verify that you meet all eligibility criteria
  3. Document Preparation: Gather all required documents
  4. Application Submission: Apply through:
    • Online portals
    • Common Service Centers (CSCs)
    • Local agriculture department offices
    • Banks (for credit schemes)
  5. Application Tracking: Keep the acknowledgment number for future reference
  6. Verification Process: Officials may conduct physical verification
  7. Approval & Benefit Transfer: Benefits are directly transferred to bank accounts

If you need assistance with applying for schemes, you can contact:

  • Kisan Call Center: 1800-180-1551 (Toll Free)
  • Block Agriculture Office: Your nearest block level agriculture office
  • Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK): Agricultural extension centers in your district
  • Common Service Centers (CSCs): Digital service centers in rural areas
  • AgriX Assistance: Submit your query through our contact form for personalized guidance
